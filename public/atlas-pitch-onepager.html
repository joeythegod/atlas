The Problem

Trip planning is still broken

Travelers juggle 8+ tabs — Google Flights, Booking.com, TripAdvisor, Viator — to manually stitch together a trip. The result: hours wasted, decisions made under stress, and still no confidence the plan makes sense.

8+ tabs avg.
4h avg. planning
90% AI planners fail logistics
The Solution

One agent. Every choice. Zero tabs.

Atlas is an AI travel agent that turns trip planning into a choosing game. Input your destination, dates, and must-sees — Claude generates your options. You pick from curated cards. The agent validates logistics. Done.

No chat box. No form overload. Just choices.

Game-like choosing UX

Card-based decision flow instead of a chat box. Every choice feels deliberate — not overwhelming. Judges call it "Duolingo for travel planning."

🤖

Claude agent orchestration

Not just a text generator. Claude uses structured tool calls to generate typed JSON options and validate logistics conflicts before you commit.

🚫

No hallucinated plans

The agent can't auto-commit to a bad itinerary. Humans confirm at every step. If your flight lands after hotel check-in closes, Atlas catches it.
